Vacancies in the Ministry of Health of Seychelles

Date Published : 13-08-2014

Applications are invited from qualified candidates who wish to work in the Republic of Seychelles on a two year contract basis.

II.    AGE

Candidates should be between 25 and 63 years by the closing date for the submission of applications.

 

III.  QUALIFICATIONS

Candidates should possess the required qualifications as mentioned below:

IV    APPOINTMENT, DUTIES AND CONDITIONS OF WORK

Selected candidates will be offered employment on a two year contract.  The duties and responsibilities are available in the download section (http://www.mauritiusjobs.mu/downloads)

V     SALARY AND REMUNERATION

The monthly salary will be in Seychelles Rupees as spelt out at paragraph III above and will commensurate with qualifications and experience.  In addition, an Inducement Allowance, over and above the personal salary will be paid.  The salary and allowances are subject to 15% contribution of Income Tax.  Further details are available on the website of the Ministry.

 

VI    MODE OF APPLICATION

1.    Qualified candidates should submit their applications on the prescribed forms, which may be obtained either from the Enquiry Counter of the Ministry of Labour,  Industrial Relations and Employment (Employment Division) located on the 11th Floor,  Sterling House, Lislet Geoffroy Street, Port Louis or the Employment Information Centres or the website of the Ministry (http://www.mauritiusjobs.mu/downloads).

2.    The application form together with the curriculum vitae and duly certified copies of   certificates should be forwarded to the address at paragraph (VIII).

3.    The post applied for should be clearly marked on the top left hand corner of the envelope.

4.    Qualifications obtained after the closing date for the submission of application will not be accepted.

5.    Incomplete, inadequate or inaccurate filling of the application form or failure to submit the Curriculum Vitae and duly certified copies of certificates may entail elimination of the applicant.

6.    Applications received after the closing date and time mentioned in the advertisement will not be considered.

 

VII  MODE OF SELECTION/RECRUITMENT

All applications received will be forwarded to the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Seychelles for selection and recruitment.

 

VIII CLOSING DATE OF APPLICATION

The closing date for submission of applications is 02 September 2014 at latest by 16 00 hours.
