Visit of Mr Frederic Nicolas of Pole Emploi Reunion

Date Published : 05-04-2016

 

In the context of the implementation of the ‘Convention Specifique d’Accompagnement a la Restructuration des Service Public de l’Emploi de la Republique de Maurice’, Mr Frederic NICOLAS, "Directeur de la Strategie, de la Cooperation et du Partenariat" Pole Emploi, Reunion is on a mission in our Ministry from 04 April to 07 April 2016.

Mr Frederic NICOLAS is one among the experts from Pole Emploi who will assist the Employment  Service among others to:

1)    Define and develop the Strategic plan of the National Employment Agency (NEA)

2)    Define services to be provided in the National Employment Agency

3)    Propose recommendations on the amendments to be made in the existing Legislation for the setting up of a National Employment Agency.
