VACANCIES AT THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH IN THE REPUBLIC OF BOTSWANA

Date Published : 22-01-2016

I       Applications are invited from qualified and registered candidates who wish to work in the Republic of Botswana on a two to three years contract basis for the following posts.

 

SN

 

POST

i

 

Senior Consultant

ii

 

Consultant

iii

 

Medical Specialist

iv

 

Chief Medical Officer

v

 

Principal Medical Officer

vi

 

Senior Medical Officer

vii

 

Medical Officer

 

II     QUALIFICATIONS

 

            Candidates should possess the required medical qualifications relevant to their respective field of activities.

 

III    APPOINTMENT, SALARIES, DUTIES AND CONDITIONS OF WORK

 

        Selected candidates will be offered employment on a 24-36 months contract commencing upon assumption of duty date.  The monthly salary will be in US dollar and will commensurate with qualifications and experience.  In addition, an Expatriate Allowance ranging between 1% - 20% depending on the salary scale, over and above the personal salary will be paid.  Further details are available on the download section of the website of the Ministry of Labour, Industrial Relations, Employment and Training: www.mauritiusjobs.mu .

 

 

IV    MODE OF APPLICATION

 

1.    Qualified candidates should submit their applications solely through the website of    the Ministry www.mauritiusjobs.mu .

2.    They should register as jobseeker and upload their Curriculum Vitae and their  certified copies of certificates relevant to the job applied for.

3.    Qualifications obtained after the closing date for the submission of application will not be accepted.

4.    Incomplete, inadequate or inaccurate filling of the registration form or failure to submit the Curriculum Vitae and duly certified copies of certificates may entail elimination of the applicant.

5.    Applications received after the closing date and time mentioned in the advertisement will not be considered.

 

V     MODE OF SELECTION/RECRUITMENT

 

All applications received will be forwarded to the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Botswana for selection and recruitment.

 

VI    CLOSING DATE OF APPLICATION

 

The closing date for submission of applications is 29 th February 2016 at latest by 16 00 hours.

 

VII   QUERY

 

        For any further information, please call on 405 0129/405 0127.
